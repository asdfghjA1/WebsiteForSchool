NAMDANG

The Namdang house upholds its motto “Dare to Excel”
in innumerable ways possible, but throughout we only
believe in the process of excelling. The house does not strive to
create perfection out of the children it fosters, but to improve the self
they are today into being somebody better. This process requires
tenacity and dedication which can be seen brimming in the eyes of
every student from Namdang. The burning desire to give their best
in everything is what unites the house, and they are aware that
together they can scale any mountain of difficulty that life throws at them.
