Academics



At Namdang, academics is priortised over any pursuit of a student. The students are accepted as they are and are allowed to improve on their academic fields in their own pace. The students of Namdang too believe in the process of improvement till they reach excellence, for this the house caters each student according to their needs and ways they are most comfortable with. The house-mates believe in peer-coaching and peer-teaching as this creates a very comfortable environment for them to learn. As a result there is visible excellence in academics by the students of Namdang as their scoresheet speaks for themselves.
